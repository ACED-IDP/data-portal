About CoVD Map

A data visualization and analytics platform for tracking the spread and impact of the COVID-19 pandemic.

CoVD Map is being developed as an open source project by the Southern Illinois University, Carbondale and the Center for Computational Science Research Inc., Open Commons Consortium, with funding support from the Walder Foundation.
